AI Oral Health Analyzer

Project Goal

The goal of this project is to provide quick and easy oral health diagnosis to those who cannot afford to go to a dentist, particularly in countries where there is no universal health coverage, and those who live far away from dental service providers. The smart probe is small, easy to use, and can easily be integrated with your current oral care routine. It will take pictures of your mouth and use those to analyze your brushing habits and provide you with personalized feedback on your oral health The device uses cutting-edge imaging technology to take detailed pictures of your oral environment. This allows you to analyze your dental surfaces, the health of your gum tissue, and the distribution of plaque. By integrating AI algorithms, you can assess your brushing techniques in real-time, identify areas of improvement, and provide personalized recommendations to improve your oral hygiene practice.
